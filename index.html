STEPHEN CHAVEZ

Biker, Runner, and Pastor

One of the things I appreciate most about Jesus Christ was His ability to defy convention and live his own life, even though many considered it scandalous.

I haven’t tried to be scandalous, but I have tried to defy convention about what it means to be a pastor, and by extension, a Christian. For me, Christians aren’t just people who ascribe to a system of beliefs -- they embrace life and accept its many challenges.
